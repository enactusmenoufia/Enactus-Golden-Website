Smart Labels

About the Project

The Smart Labels project is revolutionizing fruit distribution in Egypt by addressing the critical challenge of high fruit deterioration rates. By leveraging innovative labeling technology, the project extends the shelf life of fruits, reduces food waste, and creates economic benefits for farmers and exporters. The initiative has successfully piloted across major export stations and aims to transform the fresh produce packaging market in Egypt.

Key Features

  • Economic Benefits
  • Waste reduction
  • Future-Ready Technology
