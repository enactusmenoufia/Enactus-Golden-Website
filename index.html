About Enactus Menofia

Enactus Menoufia University is a student-led organization dedicated to using entrepreneurial action to create positive social and environmental impact.

As part of the global Enactus network, Enactus Menoufia empowers students to develop projects that address local challenges and contribute to sustainable development.

The team comprises students, academic professionals, and business leaders who collaborate to transform lives and shape a better, more sustainable world. Their mission emphasizes entrepreneurial innovation, action, and a sense of collective responsibility.

Vision & Mission
