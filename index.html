About Enactus Menofia

Enactus Menofia is a student-led organization at Menofia University dedicated to creating positive social, environmental, and economic impact through entrepreneurial projects.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To inspire and empower students and communities by fostering innovative, sustainable solutions.

Our Mission
